Working out

"Mens sana in corpore sano" - Latin for "a healthy mind in a healthy body". Now ain't this a good saying?!

As I got older and older, I switched from unhealthy snacks, sandwiches (still can't turn down a good burger lol), candy & other similar foods, into, salads (hated them as a youngster), veggies, fruits, protein-rich foods etc. Now, my eye for aesthetics accompanied by my will for a healthy life, resulted in me willing to work out. I can't just look at the mirror and be satisfied with what I see because I know that everything can be even better than it is now, not only body related but in every aspect of life. Man, I love working out... And I think that everyone should. You don't have to be a massive sized "chiffonier", not being able to scratch your own back, but being physically (don't forget psychically) active brings you numerous health benefits.
